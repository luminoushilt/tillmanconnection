Family History

THE TILLMAN LINAGE

Linking Generations One Reunion at a Time

We are in search of descendants of Russell (b 1823)and Alex (b 1830) Tillman. Both were freed slaves owned by the Samuel Herring family. They were known as the Herring brothers as freed men changed their name to Tillman. Russell had 15 children and Alex 5 children. The descendants of which we are in search of.

With our Mississippi roots many migrated to the north and beyond. An entire generation of mine (picture1)was a part of that great migration. Myself (Sullivan Montgomery) I live in Seattle Washington and born in St. Louis Missouri.
